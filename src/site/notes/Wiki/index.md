# Wiki

This is a knowledge base for my PhD research on homelessness, housing systems, and housing policy in Australia. A librarian process maintains it — reading sources, extracting key ideas, keeping things linked.

Worth saying upfront: being here doesn't mean I agree with it. I'm documenting ideas and arguments I'm actively critiquing alongside ones I find genuinely useful. The wiki covers both.

Everything is also a work in progress. Articles get updated as new sources come in, and every factual claim should trace back to one. If something isn't sourced, treat it as provisional.

---

## Navigating

Use the file tree or search to find articles. The local graph on any article shows how concepts connect to each other. Backlinks at the bottom show what else references it.

Topics are grouped into folders. New ones appear as the wiki grows.
